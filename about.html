About KPC Buddhist Temple

KPC Buddhist Relief is a branch of Kunzang Palyul Choling Buddhist Temple. Their mission is to help relieve the suffering of individuals and communities. Their objectives are to help reduce suffering by offering humanitarian aid, addressing food insecurity, offering compassion for animals, and helping heal the Earth. Their approach is based on a Buddhist principle of interdependence and interconnectedness. This principle means that even our survival depends on others and a need for love lies at the core of every individual. KPC values their sense of responsibility and sincere concern for the welfare of others. Some strategies and tactics they use to help provide care for others are through partnerships that support communities with food and other necessities. They also provide condiments and spices that can help individuals turn food into a comforting meal. They have a Giving Garden to address food insecurity that produce fresh fruits and vegetables. KPC also has a barnyard of animals to inspire animal compassion and is actively involved with creating a fully local environment. KPC aims to help all those suffering, showing that they are welcoming and inclusive of all individuals and communities trying to help as many people as possible as this is what they value most.
